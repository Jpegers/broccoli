Frequently asked questions

Broccoli is an automated yield farming aggregator on SUI blockchain. We enable users to earn competitive yields on their stablecoins through an automated process. By utilizing a single yield-bearing token, we streamline investments and maximize yield opportunities within the SUI DeFi ecosystem.
The yield is generated by automated optimization algorithms that intelligently route stablecoin lending, constantly rebalancing funds to secure the highest rates.
Yield is automatically accrued and reflects in the value of the BRO token held in your wallet. When you redeem your BRO token, you receive back the initial stablecoin you deposited and the yield accrued.
You can withdraw at any time without any lockup period. The process is designed to be straightforward and flexible to meet users' needs.
You can obtain BRO by minting directly on our website.
There are always risks when interacting with DeFi. The underlying stablecoins can depeg. A significant exploit can occur to Broccoli or the pools that Broccoli operates within.
We employ risk management strategies, including diversification across different yield opportunities and continuous monitoring of the platforms' performance to mitigate potential risks.
